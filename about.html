About Me


I began my programming journey in 2016 during my freshman year of Highschool. Technology had always interested me before but I never had the opportunity to fully immerse myself in the field. I began through the first language I started to teach myself, Python.

I continued my early stages of education in Python up until I was introduced to Java in a Computer Science class taught at my Highschool. It was through this class that I learned most of my conceptual programming knowledge.

After becoming comfortable with Java, I shifted to the more modernly popularized high level language, Javascript. I began making my own applicable projects with Javascript due to its user-friendly syntax and near limitless capabilities. It remains my language of choice to this day.
